Chicken Alfredo

Cheap, Tasty, and easy Psuedo-Italian Food!

I am getting bored of coming up with stuff to write. It's chicken alfredo. Enough said.

Ingredients

  • 1 box of fettucine noodles, preferably house brand
  • 1 jar off the shelf alfredo sauce
  • 2 chicken breasts from my bulk chicken recipe
  • Shredded parmesian cheese

Steps

  1. Cut precooked chicken breasts into slices or chunks. Set aside.
  2. Fill a pot with water and bring it to a boil on the stovetop.
  3. Once the water is boiling, pour the whole box of noodles into the boiling water. Cook until noodles are completely soft.
  4. Strain the water from the noodles and add back into pot. Turn heat down to low. Pour in jar of sauce and the sliced/chunked chicken breasts. Mix thoroughly.
  5. Serve with shredded parmesian topping.

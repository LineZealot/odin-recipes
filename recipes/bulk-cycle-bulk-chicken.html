Bulk Chicken for Bulk Cycle

Bland but Cheap Protein

Get swole and subject your taste buds to utter mediocrity with this basic reecipe.

Ingredients

  • As much chicken breast as your money can buy.
  • (Optional) Salt, pepper, or any other seasoning.

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Lay as many chicken breasts as you can evenly space on a glass or ceramic baking pan.
  3. (Optional) Sprinkle desired seasoning over top of the chicken breasts.
  4. Put in oven and set timer for 22 to 26 minutes depending on size of chicken breasts
  5. Remove when done and let cool
  6. That's literally it. Congratulations you are now a chad!
